Arsenal vs Bodo Gimt

Arsenal have travelled to Norway for the third round of fixtures in this season's Europa League group stage. The Gunners beat their opponents Bodo/Glimt 3-0 at the Emirates Stadium with little reason for concern. That said, Bodo/Glimt are notorious at their home ground for taking some big scalps here. They notoriously put six past Jose Mourinho's Roma side, who would go on to win the UEFA Conference League that same season. Worse for Arsenal, the pitch at the Aspmyra Stadium is artificial. These pitches are infamous for having a reputation for an increased risk of injury to players not used to playing on an unfamiliar surface. Arsenal could do without further squad issues, having lost Oleksandr Zinchenko, Mohamed Elneny and Emile Smith Rowe. Gabriel Jesus is also likely to be missing having been rested by Mikel Arteta ahead of the Premier League clash with Leeds United this weekend. football.london has you covered for this one with Kaya Kaynak in place in Bodo and Tom Canton running the live commentary of the game, ensuring you miss nothing. Plus, we bring you glimpses of the fan experience in Norway as the Gunners hope to claim all three points.
